About

The Foundation for Gaming Deconstruction and Narrative Analysis (FGDNA) is dedicated to uncovering hidden meanings, alternative interpretations, and deeper truths within video game lore. By blending psychology, mythology, and media analysis, we strive to push players to question widely accepted storylines. Our goal isn’t just to entertain, it’s to spark critical discussions about the narratives we consume and how they influence our perception of storytelling.
