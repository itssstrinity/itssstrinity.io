Welcome to The Hidden Truth Behind Mario’s Quest, where we uncover the untold story lurking beneath the surface of one of gaming’s most beloved franchises. What if Mario’s adventures weren’t about heroism, but instead, an endless cycle of addiction and deception? This project explores the radical reinterpretation of Mario’s journey—not as a noble rescue mission, but as an insidious pursuit of power fueled by the mysterious “Rollin Refresh” soda. Join us as we challenge conventional gaming narratives and reveal the unseen forces shaping the Mushroom Kingdom.
